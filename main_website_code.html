Types of Material
  • PLA - Most common filiment, is the only filiment that doesn't need a heated build plate, easy to print, very strong but brittle, melts in sunlight, around $20 per kg
  • ABS - As strong as PLA but way less brittle and heat resistant, toxic when printing, hard to print, around $20 per kg
  • TPU - Very flexible and strong, not very easy to print, around $40 per kg
  • PETG - As strong and wear resistant as ABS but is as easy to print as PLA, around $20 per kg
  • Nylon - Not as strong as PLA, extremly wear resistant, around $35 per kg
  • NylonX - Nylon infused with carbon fiber, as wear resistant as nylon but as strong as carbon fiber, second strongest filiment that isn't metal around $58 per half kg
  • NylonG - Nylon infused with glass shards, very inpact resistant, good at keeping shape under stress, around $64 per half kg
  • Polycarbonate - Strongest, most heat resistant and wear resistant filiment that isn't metal, Very hard to print, only has one color, around $90 per kg
  • NinjaFlex - As strong as TPU and is the most flexible, is basicly rubber, around $55 per half kg
  • markforged tool steel - metal filiment, extremly stong, $230 per 200cc roll
  • markforged copper - metal filiment, high thermal and electrical conductivity, around $300 per 200cc roll
  • markforged stainless steel - metal filiment, stong, corrosion resistant, good at keeping shape under huge stress, $130 per 200cc roll
  • markforged kevlar - most flexible markforged material, best abrasion resistance, high inpact strength, around $300 per 150cc roll
  • markforged fiberglass - very stong, good for reinforcing strength of other things, around $230 per 150cc roll
  • markforged carbon fiber - extremly strong and extremly light, around $450 per 150cc roll
